Cyclone Sector-Wise Impact Analysis

Bangladesh - Cyclone Impact by Sector

This heatmap highlights the impact of storm surges and wind from cyclones across various sectors in Bangladesh. The energy sector, particularly electricity generation and wireless services, appears to be the most affected.

Japan - Cyclone Impact by Sector

In Japan, cyclone-related wind damage is significantly concentrated in electricity generation, telecommunications, and commercial sectors. This shows the critical infrastructure vulnerability to cyclonic hazards.

Philippines - Cyclone Impact by Sector

The Philippines data shows broader distribution of damage across both public and industrial sectors. Telecommunications and power infrastructure show considerable exposure to wind events.

USA - Cyclone Impact by Sector

In the USA, wind damage from cyclones significantly affects the generation and distribution of electricity. Additionally, road infrastructure and wireline communication services are heavily impacted.
